# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
`jooservices/laravel-events` community a harassment-free experience for
everyone.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes:

- demonstrating empathy and kindness
- being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- accepting responsibility and learning from mistakes
- focusing on what is best for the project community

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances
- insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- public or private harassment
- publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to [admin@jooservices.com](mailto:admin@jooservices.com).

Maintainers will review reports promptly and fairly while respecting privacy and
security to the extent reasonably possible.
